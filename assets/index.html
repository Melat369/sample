Learn the Fundamentals

Our classes offer a comprehensive introduction to the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

You will learn the basic building blocks of web development, including the structure of a webpage, styling elements, and creating interactive elements. This includes understanding how to use HTML to create the structure of a webpage, how to use CSS to style it and make it visually appealing, and how to use JavaScript to make the page interactive. These classes are an essential foundation for anyone looking to learn web development and will give you a solid understanding of the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Read More

Learn React

Our classes offer a comprehensive introduction to the fundamentals of React.

You will learn the basics of React, including how to create reusable components, how to manage the state of your application and how to use React's virtual DOM to improve the performance of your application. These classes will give you a solid understanding of the basics of React and prepare you to build dynamic and interactive web applications with React.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our primary location is situated in the city of Bahirdar, Ethiopia. Specifically, we are located within the grounds of Poly Campus. As we continue to grow and expand our operations, we have plans to open additional offices in various locations throughout the country in the near future, so we can serve more clients and continue to make our services more accessible to people from all over Ethiopia.

The expense for participating in our classes is contingent on the format of the class. Our online classes are priced at Birr 3000, our hybrid classes are priced at Birr 5000, and our in-person classes are priced at Birr 7000. We understand that some individuals may require financial assistance and we do offer different payment options and financial aid for those who are eligible. If you have any questions about the pricing of our classes or need further information, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Prior knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and React is not a pre-requisite for attending our classes. However, having basic computer skills, such as the ability to navigate the internet and work with files, is necessary. Our classes are designed to teach the fundamentals of these subjects, starting from scratch and progressing through the material. While having some prior knowledge of these subjects may be beneficial, it is not a requirement for participating in our classes. We will guide you through the basics and help you build on that foundation throughout the course.

The length of time it takes to learn the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and React can vary depending on your prior experience and the amount of time you are able to dedicate to learning. However, on average, it can take anywhere from 4-6 weeks to learn the basics of HTML and CSS, and an additional 2-4 weeks to learn the basics of Javascript. React can take around 1-2 months to learn the basics. However, it's important to remember that true proficiency in these technologies takes ongoing practice and experience. We recommend you to join our online classes which are designed to help you learn at your own pace.

Yes, we do offer job assistance to our students. Our classes provide various resources and career services to help students find job opportunities in their field of study. This includes resume building, interview preparation, and networking opportunities with industry professionals. We also have a dedicated career services team that works closely with students to help them find job opportunities that match their skills and interests. We understand that getting a job after completing a course is important, that's why we put effort to help our graduates to find a suitable job.
